OPS Plan Overview

Fiscal year internal help desk operations plan

Introduction

  • Hi I am Lovedeep Singh

Reason for this plan

  • Enhance help desk efficiency and customer support via improved operations.

Plan Purpose

  • Guide operations; enhance help desk performance.
  • Mission: Deliver efficient service, delight customers with timely solutions.
  • Vision: Empower future operations; innovate help desk efficiency endlessly.

SMART Goals & Benefits

  • Goal 1: Increase ticket resolution by 20% this fiscal year.
    Benefit: Faster support response saves time and enhances productivity.
  • Goal 2: Reduce average response time to under five minutes.
    Benefit: Quick replies boost satisfaction and operational efficiency.
  • Goal 3: Achieve 90% positive feedback from service surveys.
    Benefit: Improved service quality ensures loyal customer satisfaction.
  • Goal 4: Implement automated ticket sorting within six months.
    Benefit: Automation streamlines workflow, reducing waiting times.
  • Goal 5: Train staff quarterly to enhance technical expertise.
    Benefit: Skilled staff improve problem-solving and user satisfaction.
